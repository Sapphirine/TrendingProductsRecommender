Trending Products Recommender for E-Commerce

Alex Dziena, Jaewon Lee, and Johan Sulaiman

Department of Computer Science
Columbia University

In a multi-platform e-commerce business, revenue growth is highly correlated with superior product selection and low prices. This paper describes a Trending Products application that provides up-to-date category-specific recommendations, by scraping Twitter and potentially other public news sources for product announcements, product reviews, product promotions, and other product-related articles, along with their accompanying social media reactions.
